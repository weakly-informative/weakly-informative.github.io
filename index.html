I am a fresh PhD out of AaltoPML . I am interested in Bayesian inference, deep learning and also the intersecetion of both. My recent work revolves around graph neural networks and Bayesian inference for distributed/multi-source data.

Follow me on twitter @wkly_infrmtive for somewhat regular updates and for occasional bad jokes. You can also contact me through first.last@aalto.fi
